# Captain Key Largo: design direction

## Step 2 diagnosis (old site: epstein23.wixsite.com/website)
1. Book Online page is untouched Wix travel-template placeholder content ($19.99 "Corporate Travel Consultation"): the primary CTA path actively embarrasses the business.
2. Free wixsite subdomain + Wix ad banner; only 1 page indexed; zero ranking potential.
3. No logo (nav image was a phone screenshot), no meta description, empty LocalBusiness schema, phone not tel-linked, zero social proof, typos, 2018 copyright.
4. Worth preserving: "Fish With A Local" tagline, the since-1976 / Bob Epstein legacy, the genuinely unusual service breadth (night trips, lobster/shrimp runs, bowfishing, dine-by-water barhop), the high-res sunset/boat/lobster photos.

## Step 3 competitive notes
Keys charter sites cluster around: blue/teal palettes, Oswald/Anton headline fonts, hero sliders, "half day/full day" pricing tables, FishingBooker booking widgets. Almost none sell night fishing or non-fishing trips as a first-class story. Differentiation: own the clock (dawn-to-dark) rather than the species list, and own the "local since 1976" persona.

## Design Direction Declaration
Workboat-honest and local: navy of the bay at dusk, sun-bleached sand, one coral accent taken from Brian's own sunset photo. The signature element is the Dawn-to-Dark rail, a time-of-day trip architecture (First Light / Full Sun / Golden Hour / After Dark) that could only belong to a one-captain operation that actually fishes all 24 hours. Deliberately avoided: species-grid cards, pricing tables with invented numbers, teal-blue charter cliches, and any luxury styling that would collide with the Poppa Wahoo sister brand.

## Section architecture and structural variation
1. Hero: full-bleed action photo, bottom-aligned copy (photo-led).
2. Statement: asymmetric 2-col headline/body (no photo, no cards).
3. Dawn-to-Dark rail (signature): 3-col rail/copy/photo bands with alternating photo side and a sticky time cell; distinct from everything else on the page.
4. Boat: split photo/dark-panel band with spec list (first inverted section).
5. Captain: light 2-col portrait + timeline marks (photo-tag overlap element crosses the image edge).
6. FAQ: 0.42/0.58 split intro + accordion.
7. Contact: dark 2-col form + info rail.
No two consecutive sections share a structure; small-caps kicker used on 4 anchor points (captain/faq/contact/boat band) with hero and statement and rail leading without one.

## Blacklist temptations and avoidance
- Identical card grid: replaced trip cards entirely with the time-rail bands.
- SaaS stats bar: credentials live in the hero cred-row and the captain timeline instead.
- Generic hero headline: H1 carries Key Largo + "with a local" + first-light-to-after-dark differentiator.
- Em dashes: zero (verified by grep).
- Transparent nav: solid sand from load.
